[h1] Confessions of Heresy Mod [/h1]

Confessions of Heresy aims to make up for holes in the vanilla terraria experience and build upon them, whilst adding its own experience to the mix

Additions include:
[list]
[*] Rework of the Blood Moon to be more similar to other events
[*] 3 New Bosses
[*] A New Micro-Biome
[*] Proper Hardmode Cave Exploration
[*] 50+ Unique Weapons
[*] 25+ New Accessories
[*] A Mage Subclass
[*] Probably some more stuff
[/list]

The mod is in early development so expect bugs.
